Frequently Asked Questions

What is the URL shortener feature?

The URL shortener feature allows users to shorten long URLs into shorter, more manageable ones.

How does the QR code feature work?

The QR code feature generates QR codes based on user input, typically URLs or other data.

What insights does the analytics feature provide?

The analytics feature provides insights into user interactions with shortened URLs or QR codes, including metrics like clicks, geographic location, devices used, and more.
